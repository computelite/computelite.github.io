Privacy Policy

Effective Date : 01 Nov 2024

At ComputeLite, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We do not use any third-party cookies, and we do not collect any personal information directly from you. The only data we collect is technical information that our server automatically receives from your browser, which may include:

• IP Address : Used to identify the geographical region from which you are accessing our website.

• Browser Type and Version : Helps us ensure our website is compatible with different browsers.

• Operating System : Ensures compatibility and helps us troubleshoot any platform-specific issues.

• Timestamp : The date and time when you accessed our site.

• Referral URL : The link that directed you to our website (if applicable).

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it, and we do not use tracking technologies such as third-party cookies.

Use of Information

The information collected by our server is used for the following purposes:

• Performance and Security : To monitor and improve the performance and security of our website.

• Troubleshooting : To identify and fix any issues with the website.

• Analytics : To understand general usage patterns and ensure the website is optimized for all visitors.

Data Retention

We retain server logs containing the above technical information for a limited period to monitor website performance and for security purposes. After that period, the logs are securely deleted.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
